About Us

Our Mission

No Hate Canada is a national movement dedicated to combating hate, fostering inclusion, and building resilient communities across the country. Launched in August 2023, our mission is to unite Canadians in action against discrimination, support those affected by hate, and champion equality as a core value. We embrace all beliefs, remaining steadfastly non-partisan and independent of any political affiliation. Our focus is on unity and relentless advocacy to create a safer Canada where every individual is valued and protected.

Our History

Since our inception in August 2023, No Hate Canada has grown into a powerful force for change. By June 24, 2025, we have mobilized thousands of supporters, launched impactful campaigns, and partnered with diverse organizations to address hate at its roots. Key milestones include our first national rally in October 2023 and the establishment of community support hubs in major cities by mid-2024.

Contact Us

For media inquiries and partnerships, reach out at info@nohatecanada.org. Join us in building a hate-free Canada.
